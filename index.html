Who or what is CanIBringMyDoggo?

We’re a couple currently living in London (UK) and I particularly got fed up with not knowing whether or not it is possible to bring our two (small) dogs with us when going anywhere, be that to a pub, a restaurant or hotel. Travelling is especially challenging at times, in particular with public transport. Want to know more about me/us, get in touch (and also read our Mission).
